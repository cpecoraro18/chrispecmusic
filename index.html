About

Chris Pecoraro is a professional upright and electric bassist with 10+ of experience in studio recording and live performance. He plays both upright and electric bass and has worked in jazz, soul, blues, theater, orchestral, and modern pop/rock settings.

Chris is currently a member of the Sean McKee Band, 2024 Chicago Blues Challenge Winners. He is always excited to connect with other artists and contribute to new projects.
