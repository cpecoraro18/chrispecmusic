About

Chris Pecoraro is a professional upright and electric bassist with over a decade of experience in studio recording and live performance across Chicago and around the world.

He’s currently a member of the Sean McKee Band, 2024 Chicago Blues Challenge Winners. Chris is always excited to connect with other artists and contribute to new projects.
